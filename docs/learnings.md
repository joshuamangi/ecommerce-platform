# Learnings

- The whole point of Computer science is to find solutions and implement them.
- These solutions usually have **tradeoffs**.
- These solutions usually have **cause and effect**
- These solutions and problem usually have a certain **flow** to them
- Someone needs to be deeply a part of the system.
- Someone needs a database of possible problems and their solutions. To be aware of most possible prooblems
- Understanding building for scale and the impact of time and space complexities
- Consider what is for and what is against. How would it stack up with and without the change. **Benefit vs Cost Analysis**
- Being able to understand the key costs of having or not having implemented a solution.
- Transfer things from the head to how it is in real life by writing down
- It is not just about learning the strategies, but the need and implications
- The price is what you pay for having or not having something
